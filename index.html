Get excited for stoping hate Speech!

Create a campaign profile picture with our theme. How better to show your anticipation for hate speech? This is how you can create your Campaign Profile Picture:

  1. Locate your profile picture on your computer, or download your profile picture from e.g. Facebook (right-click and choose "Save As...").
  2. Select your picture below, and check the overlay. Square pictures work best, as the picture will automatically be cropped to be square.
  3. Download your brand new picture (right-click and choose "Save As...") and set it on the social networks of your choice.
